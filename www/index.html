Just Sharing

A phrase meaning “Here's what worked for me in my context, but I recognize that your context is different”

Where “my context” means

  • business landscape
  • technology domain
  • personal preferences
  • etc.

I am saying

  • this worked for us
  • this is possible (there is at least one example where it worked)
  • you may have success with something similar

I'm not saying

  • You should do this
  • Your problem is the same as mine
  • This will work for you
  • You'll get the same results if you do the same thing
